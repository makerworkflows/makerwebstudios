## Usage Rules

1. **#FF6B00 is the official brand orange** -- use on website, proposals, and all new digital assets
2. **Navy + Orange** is the core brand pairing -- use on all client deliverables
3. **Cover pages**: Navy background with white text and orange accent elements
4. **Section headers**: Navy text with orange underline or label
5. **Tables**: Navy header row, alternating white/gray-50 body rows
6. **CTAs and highlights**: Orange for action items, links, and emphasis
7. **Body text**: Gray 600-800 depending on hierarchy
8. **Footers**: Gray 500 text with orange top border
9. **Audit PDFs**: Use #D4872C (warmer tone optimized for print/PDF rendering)
