# Rex Cortex — Claude Desktop Setup Guide
*Maker Web Studios | Generated 2026-03-29*

---

## What You Have

This ZIP contains your **Rex Cortex Desktop Package** — a pre-configured
knowledge base for Claude Desktop (Cowork). It gives Claude everything it needs to know
about you, your business, your voice, and your priorities.

---

## Step 1 — Unzip

Unzip this file anywhere on your computer. A good location:
`~/Documents/Rex Cortex/`

---

## Step 2 — Download Claude Desktop

If you haven't already:
1. Go to **claude.ai/download**
2. Download the macOS app
3. Sign in with your Anthropic account (Pro required — $20/month)

---

## Step 3 — Open a Cowork Session

1. Open the Claude Desktop app
2. Click **Cowork** in the left sidebar
3. Click **Select folder** or **Add folder**
4. Navigate to and select the `Rex Cortex/` folder you unzipped

Claude will now have access to all files in this folder.

---

## Step 4 — Paste the Global Instructions

1. In Claude Desktop, go to **Settings** (gear icon)
2. Find **Custom Instructions** or **System Prompt**
3. Copy the full text from `GLOBAL INSTRUCTIONS.txt` in this folder
4. Paste it in and save

This tells Claude who it is, how to behave, and what rules to follow.

---

## Step 5 — Start Your First Session

Use this first prompt every time you open a new session:

> "Read all files in the ABOUT ME folder. Then check priorities.md.
> What should I be focused on right now, and what do you need from me
> to be most useful today?"

---

## What to Update Regularly

| File | How Often | What to Change |
|---|---|---|
| `ABOUT ME/priorities.md` | Every Monday | This week's top 3 focus areas |
| `ABOUT ME/goals.md` | Quarterly | Milestones and targets |
| `ABOUT ME/business.md` | As things change | New services, pricing, clients |
| `ABOUT ME/voice.md` | After Taste Interview | Refined voice details |
| `PROJECTS/` | Ongoing | Add new project folders as they start |

---

## What This Package Can and Can't Do

**Can do:**
- Write in your voice using your style rules
- Help you think through business decisions with your context in mind
- Draft emails, proposals, content, outlines
- Reference your templates for recurring deliverables
- Track your priorities and goals

**Cannot do (requires full Cortex installation):**
- Access Gmail, Google Drive, or ClickUp directly
- Remember things between sessions automatically
- Run code or scripts
- Edit files on your computer
