## Objection Handling

**"I already use ChatGPT."**
ChatGPT forgets you the moment the tab closes. Cortex remembers your business, your voice, your goals. It's not a better chatbot — it's a different category.

**"I'm not technical."**
You don't need to be. MWS builds and configures the whole system. You answer questions, we set it up, you start using it.

**"How is this different from just uploading a PDF to Claude?"**
A PDF is static. Cortex is a living system — it updates as your business changes, builds memory over time, connects to your actual tools, and runs automated workflows. A PDF gives Claude facts. Cortex gives it a brain.

**"What does it actually do day-to-day?"**
Drafts emails and proposals in your voice. Screens leads. Tracks priorities. Answers questions about your business without you explaining the context. Runs your onboarding checklists. Sends your weekly GBP posts. Whatever you do repeatedly — it handles it.
