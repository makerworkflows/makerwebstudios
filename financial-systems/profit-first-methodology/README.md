## What is Profit First?

### The Core Principle

Flip the traditional accounting equation:

- **Traditional**: Sales - Expenses = Profit (profit is leftover)
- **Profit First**: Sales - Profit = Expenses (profit comes first)

###

 Why It Works

**Parkinson's Law Applied to Money**: Expenses expand to fill available cash.

**Solution**: Remove cash from operating account (force scarcity) → Drives innovation and efficiency.

## MWS-Specific Simplification

### Book's 5-Account Model

1. Revenue (checking)
2. Operating (checking)
3. Profit (savings)
4. Tax (savings)
5. Owner's Pay (savings)

### Our 2-Account Model (Simplified)

**Account 1: Business Checking**
- Revenue deposits
- Operating expenses
- Owner's pay withdrawals

**Account 2: Business Savings**
- Profit reserves (untouchable)
- Tax reserves
- No debit card (behavioral lock)

**Why 2 Instead of 5?**
- Solo founder (Guillermo + Rex) - 5 accounts is overkill
- Achieves same outcome: forced scarcity drives innovation
- Can expand to 5 if/when scaling to team

## Core Concepts

### 1. Target Allocation Percentages (TAPs)

At $12K MRR (8 clients @ $1,500/mo):

| Account | % | Monthly Amount |
|---------|---|----------------|
| Profit | 5% | $600 |
| Tax | 15% | $1,800 |
| Owner's Pay | 50% | $6,000 |
| Operating | 30% | $3,600 |

**Start**: 1% profit on Day 1
**Increase**: To 5% by Month 3

### 2. Forced Scarcity = Innovation

Less money available for operations forces creative cost reduction:
- Question: "How can we keep doing what we're doing for one third of the cost?"
- Already doing this: 5 website templates, 6-prompt SEO, manual Gmail outreach
- Profit First extends this to all business expenses

### 3. Profit as Habit, Not Event

"Profit is not an event. Profit is a habit."

Start with 1% allocation even on $500 revenue → $5 profit. Build the muscle.

### 4. Behavioral Locks

Remove temptation to "borrow" from profit/tax reserves:
- No debit card on savings account
- Physical/mental barrier to accessing funds
- "Piggy bank you block yourself from stealing from"
