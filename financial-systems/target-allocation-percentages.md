## Monthly Calculations

### How to Calculate Allocations

**Formula**: Monthly Revenue × Allocation % = Transfer Amount

**Example @ $12K MRR (Stage 3)**:

1. **Profit**: $12,000 × 5% = $600
2. **Tax**: $12,000 × 15% = $1,800
3. **Owner's Pay**: $12,000 × 45% = $5,400
4. **Operating**: $12,000 × 35% = $4,200

**Verification**: $600 + $1,800 + $5,400 + $4,200 = $12,000 ✓

---

### Transfer Schedule (2x per Month)

**1st of Month**:
- Calculate allocation based on revenue from previous 2 weeks
- Transfer Profit + Tax to savings
- Note: Owner's Pay stays in checking (withdraw as needed)

**15th of Month**:
- Calculate allocation based on revenue from previous 2 weeks
- Transfer Profit + Tax to savings
- Note: Owner's Pay stays in checking (withdraw as needed)

**Example @ $12K MRR**:

| Transfer Date | Revenue (2 weeks) | Profit (5%) | Tax (15%) | Total Transfer |
|---------------|-------------------|-------------|-----------|----------------|
| March 1 | $6,000 | $300 | $900 | $1,200 |
| March 15 | $6,000 | $300 | $900 | $1,200 |
| **Monthly Total** | **$12,000** | **$600** | **$1,800** | **$2,400** |

## Adjustment Triggers

### When to Increase Profit %

**Trigger**: Revenue consistently above stage threshold for 3 months

**Example**:
- Currently at Stage 2 (3% profit, $5K-$12K MRR)
- Revenue stable at $13K-$15K for 3 months
- **Action**: Move to Stage 3 (5% profit)

---

### When to Decrease Profit % (Emergency Only)

**Trigger**: Revenue drops below stage threshold AND operating budget insufficient

**Example**:
- Currently at Stage 3 (5% profit, $12K-$25K MRR)
- Revenue drops to $8K MRR and stays there
- Operating budget at 35% ($2,800) insufficient
- **Action**: Drop to Stage 2 (3% profit), review expenses

**Important**: Never drop profit to 0%. Maintain minimum 1% to preserve habit.

## Special Situations

### Irregular Revenue (Consulting Projects)

**Problem**: Monthly revenue fluctuates ($5K one month, $20K next)

**Solution**: Use trailing 3-month average

**Example**:
- Month 1: $5K
- Month 2: $20K
- Month 3: $10K
- Average: ($5K + $20K + $10K) / 3 = $11.67K

**Apply Stage 3 TAPs** (rounded to $12K):
- Profit: 5% × $12K = $600/month
- Tax: 15% × $12K = $1,800/month
- Owner's Pay: 45% × $12K = $5,400/month
- Operating: 35% × $12K = $4,200/month

**Adjust quarterly** as average changes

---

### One-Time Windfalls

**Problem**: $50K project lands, but not recurring

**Solution**: Allocate extra to profit (save for lean months)

**Example**:
- Regular MRR: $12K
- One-time project: $50K
- Total month revenue: $62K

**Option A: Apply TAPs as usual**
- Profit: 5% × $62K = $3,100 (save for lean months)
- Tax: 15% × $62K = $9,300 (set aside)
- Owner's Pay: 45% × $62K = $27,900
- Operating: 35% × $62K = $21,700

**Option B: Bonus allocation to profit**
- Profit: 50% × $50K = $25,000 (windfall reserve)
- Regular allocation on $12K MRR as usual

**Recommendation**: Option B (treat windfall separately, avoid lifestyle inflation)
