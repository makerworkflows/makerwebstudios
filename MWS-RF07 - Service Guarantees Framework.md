# MWS-RF07 - Service Guarantees Framework

**Date:** 2026-03-14 (Updated)
**Status:** Draft — Ready to formalize into proposals
**Pricing context:** GrowthEngine $4,500 build + Care Plan $1,500/mo retainer

---

## Approved Guarantees

### 1. 30-Day Launch Guarantee
> "If your site isn't live within 30 days of kickoff, you don't pay for that month."

- Already aligned with current delivery timeline
- Removes the #1 first-time buyer fear: paying and waiting forever
- Zero additional risk — this is what we already do

### 2. PageSpeed Guarantee
> "We'll get your site to 90+ on Google PageSpeed or we keep working until it does."

- Highly specific, measurable, spec-based
- Competitors almost never promise this
- Manufacturers respect spec-based commitments — speaks their language

### 3. First Order Quality Guarantee
> "If the finished site doesn't match the approved design and scope, we rebuild the non-conforming sections at no charge."

- Already referenced in ABBA audit deck — formalize for all proposals
- ISO-style language, zero ambiguity
- Ties to approved scope document as the benchmark

### 4. 90-Day Ranking Check-In
> "If you're not seeing measurable keyword movement within 90 days, we audit why and adjust the strategy at no cost."

- NOT a ranking guarantee (Google controls rankings)
- Guarantee of effort and accountability
- Keeps us honest without promising the uncontrollable

---

## What to Avoid

**Money-back guarantees on web builds** — You can't un-deliver a website. The client already has the files, domain, and work product. Structure risk reversal around **redoing work**, not refunding it.

---

## Proposal Framing

Guarantees get their own section — one page, bold headline, plain language. No bullet-list burial.

Example copy:

> "We've done this long enough to stand behind it. If we miss the 30-day launch window, you don't pay for that month. If PageSpeed doesn't hit 90+, we keep working. No asterisks."

This signals **confidence**, not legal hedging.

---

## The Deeper Point

The most powerful risk reversal is already in our process: **the free audit**. It proves the problem with the prospect's own data before they spend a dollar. The formal guarantees above reinforce what the audit already communicates — that we're certain enough in our work to put terms on it.
