## Stage 01 Implementation Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)
**Goal**: Establish baseline metrics and systems

- [ ] Define Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
  - Manufacturer revenue range: $2M-$50M
  - Location: US-based, <250 miles from Texas (avoid non-compete)
  - Pain points: Slow website, low Google PageSpeed score
  - Decision maker: Owner, VP Sales, Marketing Director

- [ ] Build lead scoring system
  - Website speed: 0-40 (high priority), 41-70 (medium), 71+ (low)
  - Company size: Revenue indicators from LinkedIn
  - Engagement: Email opens, link clicks, replies

- [ ] Set up tracking infrastructure
  - Lead database (Google Sheets or Airtable initially)
  - Email tracking (Gmail API metrics)
  - Pipeline stages in HubSpot Free or ClickUp

### Phase 2: Outreach Scale (Weeks 3-6)
**Goal**: Reach 100 emails/day consistently

- [ ] Validate email infrastructure
  - Domain warming (if new domain)
  - Deliverability testing (MillionVerifier or similar)
  - Spam score monitoring

- [ ] A/B test email sequences
  - Test 3 subject line variations
  - Test 2 hook approaches (PageSpeed vs competition)
  - Iterate based on open/reply rates

- [ ] Build response handling system
  - Template responses for common objections
  - Discovery call scheduling automation (Calendly)
  - CRM auto-logging of interactions

### Phase 3: Conversion Optimization (Weeks 7-10)
**Goal**: Improve lead-to-client conversion rate

- [ ] Refine discovery process
  - Pre-call qualification questions
  - Site audit deliverable (free value)
  - Clear next-step proposals

- [ ] Standardize proposals
  - Template with 3 package tiers
  - Case studies and social proof
  - Fast-turnaround commitment (2-week builds)

- [ ] Implement follow-up cadence
  - Proposal sent → 2-day follow-up → 5-day follow-up → 10-day final touch
  - Automated reminders via Rex or Make.com

### Phase 4: Retention & Expansion (Weeks 11-12)
**Goal**: Convert project clients to retainer clients

- [ ] Design retainer packages
  - Maintenance tier: $500/mo (hosting, updates, monitoring)
  - Growth tier: $1,500/mo (SEO, content, monthly optimization)
  - Enterprise tier: $3,000/mo (full marketing support)

- [ ] Build client onboarding system
  - Welcome packet with next steps
  - Monthly reporting templates
  - Quarterly business reviews (QBRs)

- [ ] Create upsell triggers
  - Post-launch: SEO package offer
  - 3-month mark: Maintenance plan pitch
  - 6-month mark: Additional pages/features

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Reply Rates
**Mitigation**:
- A/B test subject lines and hooks aggressively
- Improve lead targeting (better ICP filtering)
- Add personalization (company-specific PageSpeed scores)

### Risk 2: Poor Conversion from Calls to Proposals
**Mitigation**:
- Record discovery calls, analyze objections
- Improve qualification (only call high-intent leads)
- Offer free site audit as value-add during call

### Risk 3: Client Churn After Project
**Mitigation**:
- Build retainer pitch into project deliverables
- Demonstrate ongoing value (monthly reports with metrics)
- Create "maintenance required" messaging (security, updates)

### Risk 4: Capacity Constraints
**Mitigation**:
- Use 5 layout templates to speed delivery
- Standardize processes (checklists, SOPs)
- Raise prices once at 6-8 clients to slow intake
