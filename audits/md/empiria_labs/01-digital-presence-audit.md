## Executive Summary
Empiria Labs is the only dual A2LA-accredited analytical testing laboratory in South Texas, holding ISO/IEC 17025:2017 certification for both chemistry and biological testing. Despite this formidable credential -- one that most regional competitors cannot match -- their digital presence is functionally nonexistent. The website (empirialabs.com) is a WordPress shell running the Divi theme with a single "About Us" heading and no service descriptions, contact information, certifications, or conversion pathways. For a laboratory that serves produce growers who need fast, accredited testing to clear regulatory hurdles, every day without a functioning website means sample submissions going to Bureau Veritas in Houston or Eurofins in San Antonio instead.

The gap between what Empiria Labs IS and what their website SHOWS is among the most severe we have audited. This is not a case of an outdated site -- it is a case of a site that communicates nothing. The opportunity is equally severe: with minimal competition in the RGV for accredited food safety testing, even a basic professional presence would immediately capture local search traffic that currently has nowhere to land.

## The Company
Empiria Labs, LLC is an analytical testing laboratory founded in 2019, located at 6200 E. Ramseyer Rd in Edinburg, TX. The lab specializes in pesticide residue analysis, microbiological testing, heavy metals, and mycotoxin analysis for the fruits and vegetables industry. They also perform petroleum geochemistry work including TOC, Pyrolysis, Rock Eval, and biomarker analysis. The company has 6 employees and is led by Jorge Cortes, PhD (Scientific Director) and Sarai Cortes (Operations/Business Development). They serve produce growers across the Rio Grande Valley and regulatory agencies in the USA and Latin America.

## The Credentials
A2LA accredited under ISO/IEC 17025:2017 for Chemistry (Certificate 6041.01) and Biological Testing (Certificate 6041.02). This dual accreditation makes Empiria the only lab in South Texas offering both chemistry and microbiology services under ISO 17025. They utilize LC/MS-MS, GC/MS-MS, and ICP/OES instrumentation, with extraction capabilities including ASE-350, QuEChERS, and Solid Phase Extraction.

## The Platform
WordPress CMS running Divi Theme v4.27.4 by Elegant Themes. No CDN detected. No analytics detected. Site was last modified April 11, 2025. The site uses Outfit and Roboto fonts via Google Fonts. Page structure is minimal -- effectively a blank template with no substantive content deployed.

## The Market Reality
The food safety testing market is dominated by multinational players: Bureau Veritas (Houston), Eurofins Scientific (multiple Texas locations), and SGS SA. These competitors have comprehensive websites with online sample submission portals, accreditation badges prominently displayed, detailed service catalogs organized by testing type, and strong Google presence. However, none of them are physically located in the RGV -- Empiria's proximity advantage is significant for produce growers who need fast turnaround and want to avoid shipping delays.

## Strategic Finding
Empiria Labs holds dual A2LA accreditation that most labs in the region cannot match. The website presents as if the business does not exist.
